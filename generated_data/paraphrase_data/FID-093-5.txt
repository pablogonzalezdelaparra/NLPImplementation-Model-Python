Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a transformative advancement that has become an integral part of our everyday lives and industrial processes. The rapid evolution of this technology holds the potential to bring about significant changes in a wide range of areas, including advanced industries and the daily lives of ordinary people. Artificial intelligence continuously enhances human experiences by influencing interactions and enhancing skills. Contemporary educational institutions utilize AI algorithms to track attendance using facial recognition technologies. In the future, the emergence of autonomous vehicles signifies the highest point of AI implementation, as vehicles depend solely on AI systems to navigate, identify traffic signals, and travel on roadways.
