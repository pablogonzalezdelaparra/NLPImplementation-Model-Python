Artificial Intelligence (AI) stood as a pivotal innovation deeply ingrained in both our daily routines and industrial operations. Its rapid evolution have promised transformative impacts across various sectors, from cutting-edge industries to the lives of ordinary individuals. AI constantly updated human experiences, shaping interactions and augmenting capabilities. For instance, contemporary educational institutions leveraged AI algorithms for attendance tracking via facial recognition technology. Looking ahead, the advent of autonomous vehicles represented a pinnacle of AI application, where vehicles relied entirely on AI systems for navigation, detecting traffic signals, and navigating roads.
