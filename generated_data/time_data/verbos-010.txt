Artificial intelligence (AI) was rapidly transforming various industries, including education. AI was used in educational management to enhance the learning process, improve student outcomes, and streamline administrative tasks. This research work aimed to explore the application of AI in educational management, including its benefits and challenges. The research work employed a systematic review methodology, examining the literature on AI in educational management. The study found that AI had several advantages, including improving student engagement, personalization of learning, and cost-effectiveness. However, AI also posed several challenges, such as ethical concerns, potential biases, and the need for re-skilling the workforce. The research concluded that AI had an enormous capacity to improve educational management, but it must be deployed with care and caution.
